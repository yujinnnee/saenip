Packing Service Guide

The packing service prioritizes the safety and efficiency of the goods.
We provide careful packing for damage prevention and economical packaging that minimizes volume as standard.
Unless specified otherwise, packaging will be done in a standard manner.
If you require a specific packing method or additional requests,
please detail your requests in the "Request" section during the application, and we will provide customized services.

International shipping charges are based on the larger value between weight and volume.
Saenip uses boxes that match the size of the items to offer affordable shipping fees,
and minimizes unnecessary boxes to pack everything into as few boxes as possible.
However, restricted items will be shipped separately.

Vacuum Packing

A compression packing service to reduce the volume of large items such as clothing and bedding.

Can Packaging

A can-packaging service for ensuring the safety of food items.

Special Packaging (Air Pack)

An air pack packaging service to prevent damage to fragile or low-height items.
